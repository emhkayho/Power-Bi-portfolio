# Olist E-commerce Analysis

This project analyzes the performance of Olist, a Brazilian e-commerce platform, from 2016 to 2018. The analysis covers various aspects of Olist's operations, including revenue, order volume, popular product categories, seller performance, customer behavior, and profitability. 

The project utilizes Python and Power BI for data cleaning, analysis, and visualization. The results of the analysis are presented in a report that provides insights and recommendations for Olist to improve its operations and profitability.

## Files in the Repository

- `Olist Data Cleaning.ipynb`: Jupyter notebook containing the data quality check
- 'Business Olist.pdf': a pdf of the powerBi visualisations
- `Olist Sales Report'.ppt`: a Powerpoint report presenting the findings, insights, and recommendations for Olist based on the analysis

## Insights and Recommendations

The analysis revealed several key insights about Olist's operations and performance, including:

- Olist's revenue and order volume have been steadily increasing, with peaks in August.
- Bed_bath_table, sports_leisure, and furniture_decor are the most popular product categories on Olist
- Sellers with high ratings (4 or 5) have the most sales on Olist
- Credit cards and boleto are the most commonly used payment methods on Olist
- Security_and_services and fashion_children_clothes have the highest profit margins on Olist

Based on these insights, some recommendations for Olist to improve its operations and profitability include:

- Implement targeted marketing campaigns during peak seasons to increase sales
- Encourage sellers to maintain high ratings by providing quality service
- Offer discounts or promotions on less popular products to increase their sales
- Identify opportunities to increase profitability across different product categories
